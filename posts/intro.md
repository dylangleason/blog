Hi there. This is my first blog post. Or, to be more precise, my first
_published_ blog post.

The idea of a personal blog has been knocking around in my head for a
while, and the code base for this website has been gestating
since 2021. As in 2021, the primary impetus for starting a blog is the
sudden abundance of time I found myself with due to unemployment.

I suppose a bit of an explanation is in order. I was laid off late
last year just prior to concluding a rather large project for a major
casino and resort company. While there, I was building a fairly
complex web backend for an real-money online casino. The work was
equal parts challenging, frustrating, and illuminating, and I
definitely learned a lot. Alas, for reasons beyond my control, said
company closed my division in October and I now find myself in that
familiar place again.

But, I digress. The real reason I wanted to write today was to
introduce myself and this blog. So without further ado&hellip;

My name is Dylan and, as you may have inferred from the above, I am a
software engineer. As such, I will likely be writing about software
engineering, technology and related topics. 

But, to be honest with you, it may also serve as a vehicle for me
bitching about the state of the world (which, if you haven't noticed,
is dire). Accordingly, I will likely also write about current affairs
and politics from time to time. Consider yourself warned.
