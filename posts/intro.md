Hi there. This is my first blog post. Or, to be more accurate, my first
_published_ blog post.

The idea of a personal blog has been knocking around in my head for a
while, and the code base for this website has been gestating
since 2021. As in 2021, the primary impetus for starting a blog is the
sudden abundance of time I've been granted due to recent unemployment.

I suppose a bit of an explanation is in order. I was laid off late
last year, just prior to concluding a rather large project for a major
casino and resort company. While there, I helped build a fairly
complex web backend for a real-money online casino. The work was equal
parts challenging, frustrating, and illuminating, and I definitely
learned a lot. Alas, for reasons beyond my control, said company
closed my division in October and I now find myself in that familiar
place again.

But, I digress. The real reason I wanted to write today was to
introduce myself and this blog. So without further ado:

My name is Dylan and, as you may have inferred from the above, I am a
software engineer. As such, I will likely be writing about software
engineering, technology and related topics. 

But, to be honest with you, it may also serve as a vehicle for me to
talk about life and bitch about the current state of the world. Which
is to say: I may also write about current events and politics from
time to time. So if you "don't do politics", consider yourself warned.

With that said, I hope to write a new post at least once a month.
Though I am not holding myself to such a commitment, there's obviously
a lot to talk about these days, so I think I can manage.
